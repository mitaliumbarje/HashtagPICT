BIO

PASC is the student chapter organization subsidiary of ACM. It is an organization that provides an opportunity for students to acquire proficiency in not only technical but also non-technical domains. The students can augment their skills, implement their knowledge and gain expertise in the various thought-provoking events, SIGs, workshops, and illustrious contests organized by PASC.
