BIO

PICT Art Circle is a cultural group of the college that officially represents our college at various levels. It also conducts and performs various shows on campus for the students such as FE meet, Abhivyakti, etc. Students from all classes and departments are welcome here. PICT Art Circle is a place where you start at nothing, but in time can become a master in numerous things like music, painting, acting, etc. This cultural group provides students with an enriching experience and exposure to various prestigious platforms. PICT Art Circle has a glorious past of performing excellently in the Purushottam Karandak and the Firodiya Karandak competitions. It has also left a significant mark in the Daajikaka Gaadgil Karandak and SPPU One Act Play competitions.
